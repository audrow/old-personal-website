About

I am an NSF Fellow and a Master's student in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the University of Michigan. I am part of Dr. David Remy's Robotics and Motion Laboratory, which develops legged robots and assistive robotic devices such as active prostheses and exoskeletons. Also, I am the president of and an interviewer for the robotics news organization Robots Podcast.

About

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Southern California. I am part of Maja Matarić's Interaction Lab, which focuses on socially assistive robotics.

I received my Master's and Bachelor's Degrees in Electrical Engineering from the University of Michigan and UNC Charlotte, respectively.
